Major neurotransmitter metabolites found in the gut microbiome:

1. 5-HIAA (5-hydroxyindoleacetic acid) - serotonin metabolite
2. HVA (homovanillic acid) - dopamine metabolite 
3. VMA (vanillylmandelic acid) - norepinephrine/epinephrine metabolite
4. MHPG (3-methoxy-4-hydroxyphenylglycol) - norepinephrine metabolite
5. DOPAC (3,4-dihydroxyphenylacetic acid) - dopamine metabolite
6. 3-MT (3-methoxytyramine) - dopamine metabolite
7. GABA metabolites (such as succinic semialdehyde)
8. Histamine metabolites (imidazoleacetic acid, methylhistamine)
9. Glutamate metabolites (Î±-ketoglutarate, glutamine)
10. Kynurenine pathway metabolites (from tryptophan metabolism)

# Electronic Measurement
Electrodermal Activity (EDA) / Galvanic Skin Response (GSR)

Basic principle: Measures electrical conductance between two points on the skin
Equipment:

Silver/silver chloride (Ag/AgCl) electrodes
Constant voltage source (typically 0.5V)
Current measurement circuit
